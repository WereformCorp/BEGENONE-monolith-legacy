Explicit Content Prohibited:

We support freedom of expression, but encourage respectful dialogue and a constructive approach to all opinions.

Cyber Abuse and Bullying

Cyber-bullying Prohibited:

Image/Video Based Abuse

Sharing explicit, non-consensual images or videos is strictly prohibited. This includes:

Spam, Scam, and Deceptive Content

Prohibited Activities:

External Links

External links are allowed as long as they do not lead to harmful content such as:

  • Pornographic sites

  • Sites promoting hate, terrorism, or illegal activities

Enforcement and Penalties

Violations of these rules will result in varying penalties:
